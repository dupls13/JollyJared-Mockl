Beach Rentals and Delivery NOW AT A BEACH NEAR YOU

Heading to the beach but missing the essentials to make it a perfect day? Jolly Jared has the gear you need to make the beach a blast.

Select rental items below - list quantities at checkout. Be sure you provide the name of your lodging, rental date and phone number in the "Note To Seller" box when you check out, so we know where to drop the items. Or, if you prefer, you can call us at 805-722-7177 to place an order.

Please note:

Orders must be placed before 11:00 A.M. for same day delivery. If you need to cancel an order, you must do so before 11:00 A.M. the day of the order or you will be charged the full amount of your order. Your equipment will be dropped off at your location at designated time between 10:00 A.M.-12:00 P.M. We will return at 5:00 P.M.-6:00 P.M. to pick up the equipment. Daily and weekly rentals are available.
